How will Artificial Intelligence affect the human race?

Published 3 months ago5 min read4 comments

Will Artificial Intelligence take over humans in the future? There's a talk going on around the world that every job will be replaced by Artificial Intelligence and machine learning. What will happen then? How should humans be protected if every job is replaced? Experts have been warning people since the early 1900s that AI will take over the human race, and there will be a surge in unemployment. By replacing the current employment with man-made intelligent machines, there will be a rise in robots and self-learning machines. These will take over humans in a few decades and this is the warning given by experts.

Self-learning software has come out, and this is just the beginning. With AI becoming more complicated, the Captchas and login systems have become difficult. Artificial Intelligence seems to improve day by day, and this brings a lot more jobs into automation. But the catch is, to build these kinds of software, requires human effort. This is a huge change in humanity and enhances certain tasks, and life becomes more efficient. The myth that everyone believes that robots can take over humans and rule us is unbelievable, still many tend to believe them. Let's all hope that AI brings good to humans and the efforts put by us.
